Section 04

Behind the
Workflow

01 / INTAKE
Raw Footage
Every project starts with a deep dive into your raw material — understanding the shoot, the narrative intent, and the emotional beats before a single clip is touched.
02 / ASSEMBLY
Offline Edit
Building the rough cut in Premiere Pro — pacing, structure, rhythm. This is where the story finds its spine. Multiple revisions until the heartbeat is right.
03 / MOTION
Motion Graphics
After Effects becomes the canvas for titles, transitions, overlays, and kinetic elements that elevate the visual language to a cinematic level.
04 / COLOUR
Color Grading
DaVinci Resolve transforms the footage into a painterly world. Skin tones are preserved, mood is sculpted, and every scene has a signature look.
05 / SOUND
Audio Design
Music selection, foley layering, dialogue polish, and atmospheric sound design — because 50% of cinema lives in your ears.
06 / DELIVERY
Final Export
Platform-optimised exports — 4K broadcast masters, Instagram-ready verticals, YouTube HDR. Every pixel perfect, every deadline met.
